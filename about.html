Company Profile

Gisha Freighters Uganda Ltd, a licensed clearing and forwarding company in Uganda. the company was founded in 2018 on a border of Uganda and Rwanda called Cyanika. Gisha Logistics & Tax Services is a modern logistics, clearing & forwarding, and tax education company based in Uganda. We specialize in providing efficient import and export solutions, customs clearance, and professional tax advisory services. With a customer-first approach, we ensure goods move smoothly across borders while empowering businesses with tax knowledge and compliance support.

Vision

To be the leading logistics and tax education partner in East Africa, recognized for excellence, innovation, and trust in every service we deliver.

Mission

To simplify cross-border trade and tax compliance by providing reliable, affordable, and transparent logistics, clearing, and educational services that empower businesses and individuals to succeed.

Our Services

  • Customs clearing and forwarding
  • Import and export logistics
  • Tax advisory and education services
  • Warehousing and distribution solutions
  • Business compliance and consultancy
